Commands:

(use a ; as a prefix for all commands)

search <input> Search for <input> on youtube
choose <input> Choose a song
Type a number to choose from the latest search results
Type a youtube song/playlist link to play a song/playlist
leave Leave the voice channel
stop Stop playing the current video
queue Get the list of videos in the song queue
The latest queue message will auto-update
clear Clear the queue
play <number> Play <number> from the queue (will play the first video in the queue if no <number>)
next | skip Plays the next video in the queue
remove <number> Remove <number> from the queue
repeat Put the current video on/off repeat
info Get info of <number> from the queue (will get current info if no <number>)
pause Pause the video
resume Resume the video
