Commands:

(use a ; as a prefix for all commands)

s (input) Search for (input) on youtube
c (input) Choose a song
Type a number to choose from the latest search results
Type a youtube song/playlist link to play a song/playlist
leave Leave the voice channel
queue Get the list of videos in the song queue
The latest queue command will auto-update
clear Clear the playlist
playcurrent Plays the first video in the queue
next | skip Plays the next video in the queue
pause Pause the video
resume Resumes the video
